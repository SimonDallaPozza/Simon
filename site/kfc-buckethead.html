KFC:
Buckethead Army

For quick service restaurants, summer is a must-win season. That also makes it the most competitive. So, leveraging the nation’s zeal for cricket, KFC created a unique way for Australia to show their support for the national team: by putting a bucket on their heads. The KFC Buckethead Army achieved 80% unprompted awareness, leading to KFC growing over 30% faster than the rest of the category.
